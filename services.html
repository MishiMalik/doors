SERVICES

OUR SERVICES

Does your home's entrance need a face-lift? Mindadoor is second to none when it comes to creating stunning entryways in a wide variety of styles and materials. Check out what's on offer at our busy door manufacturing factory.

1) Steel Doors

Steel doors are a popular choice for their durability and strength. They are resistant to harsh weather conditions and can withstand heavy use, making them ideal for commercial properties and homes in areas with high foot traffic.
Nothing can beat a high-quality steel door for security. So, if safety is a high priority, ask about our custom steel door solutions.

2) Wooden Doors

Wooden doors are a classic and timeless choice that offer a warm and inviting feel to any home or business. They come in a wide range of styles and finishes, from rustic to modern, allowing you to create a unique and personalized look. A well-crafted wooden door is also durable and long-lasting, and able to withstand wear and tear while maintaining their beauty over time.

3) Melamine Doors

MDF doors are an excellent choice for those looking for a cost-effective and durable option. Made from a combination of wood fibers and resin, MDF doors offer excellent stability and resistance to warping or cracking, even in humid environments. They are also low-maintenance and easy to clean, making them an ideal choice for busy households or commercial properties.

4) PVC Doors

When you are after a low-maintenance door solution that's affordable, yet stylish and functional, then look no further then our PVC door range. They are moisture resistant, making them perfect for bathrooms and kitchens, and anywhere else moisture might be present. PVC is a lightweight material that makes PVC doors a breeze to transport and install, which always works out great for your budget. Plus, they offer excellent insulation, making them a good choice for improving energy efficiency.

5) Veneer Doors

Get the beauty and warmth of a wooden door at a fraction of the price with our veneer door selection. Veneer doors are available in a wide range of finishes and styles, from classic wood stains to bold and modern colors, making them a versatile alternative for any design aesthetic. They are also low-maintenance and easy to clean, so they are a great choice for busy households or commercial properties.

6) Bathroom and Kitchen Cabinets and Furniture

Mindadoor also offers beautiful and functional cabinetry for your bathroom and kitchen. We create every piece of furniture according to your vision and exacting needs. You can be sure that only the highest quality materials go into our unique designs. Whatever your desire, from extra storage, to a fresh new look, Mindadoor can create everything from simple wall-mounted cabinets to full custom kitchen and bathroom installations. We guarantee you will get the storage space you need in a design that complements your decor.
