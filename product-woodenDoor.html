WOODEN DOOR

What are Wooden Door Models and Features? Although the variety of interior door models is increasing day by day, classic models are still popular today.

How are Wooden Door Models Produced?

Wood is a building material that has been used by people for centuries. This wood, which is found in almost all of our living spaces, can be used in interior and exterior doors according to its quality. When we say wooden door, we usually think of Furniture Door and Press Door. However, the Press Door was used in ancient times. Today, in terms of quality and durability, Press Door is one of the last door models that can be preferred. When we look at the recent years, different interior door models have been brought to our country. The most popular of these are American panel doors. Although one of the most used doors is American doors, Classic Wooden Door Models are still used in our country and in the world. Types of wooden doors basically divided into two. These doors are solid doors produced from natural timber and American panel doors with fabrication production and easy assembly.

In this article, we are concerned to wooden doors produced from natural timber. We can also say that these doors are 100% wood. Compared to other door materials, handwork is much more and the chemical substance in it is much less. Since it does not contain toxic substances, solid wooden interior doors are among the first choices of people who care about their health. Wooden Doors can be produced from different tree models. Trees with different characteristics such as Mahogany, Acacia, Pine, Teak, Oak, Beech and are used in wooden products. Each of these products has different properties. It is produced by taking into account the expectation of the user, such as keeping moisture, being durable, being hard or light. Let's examine the Features of Wooden Interior Doors.

Features of Wooden Interior Doors

When we look at the wooden interior doors in general, they are the first of the most used interior door models. We can say that it is used so much that it is a proof of its quality. People have been using wooden interior door models for a long time with their features such as being durable, natural texture, resistance to water and humidity, and easy resistance of hard impacts and scratches. In addition, its incredible harmony with every piece of furniture should not go unnoticed.

Durability: Wooden interior door models combine the hardness of a tree and the lightness of a wood. It is both hard and durable and very easy to transport and install. It does not deform even in large-scale impacts. The almost absence of chemical products in it should also catch our eye as natural durability. Wooden interior doors are one of the interior door models that can be used for many years after purchase.

Polishing and Maintenance: The maintenance of wooden interior doors is also not difficult. There is no need for maintenance other than polishing once a year and cleaning with a wet cloth every two weeks. Chemical products should not be used when cleaning wooden doors. Chemical products disrupt the structure of natural interior door models. Only soap should be used after food stains or any large stains.

Decoration: Those who are undecided in home decoration or do not want to spend time on decoration can choose wooden doors with peace of mind. Wooden doors can adapt to every colour and every model of furniture due to their structure. In addition, due to its natural texture, it adds a nice atmosphere to the environment and can make you feel refreshed.

Easy Repair: Let’s mention that it is very difficult for wooden door models to be damaged. However, don't worry if your wooden interior door gets damaged. Wooden Interior Door models are door models that can be repaired without much effort. You can contact the company you bought it from and repair it at very affordable prices.

Wooden Interior Door Prices

Wooden interior door models are one of the interior door models that maintain their popularity with their wide features and reasonable price. The best interior door model you can have in terms of price-quality is wooden doors. It is not possible for a door that is cheaper than wooden doors to have more features. For this reason, people have used wooden doors for many years and have not searched for a new door. Although there are many internal door models today, wooden interior door models have a special place.
